OMODA & JAECOO KICK OFF THE 2024 BEIJING AUTO SHOW WITH ROBOT MORNINE, J7 PHEV AND J8 PHEV

Jakarta, 22 April 2024 – OMODA & JAECOO are preparing to surprise the 2024 Beijing Auto Show which will take place on 25 April by launching a series of new breakthroughs. The innovation presented by OMODA & JAECOO will mark a new era in mobility and human-machine interaction. "We are very enthusiastic about presenting various new innovations from OMODA & JAECOO at the 2024 Beijing Auto Show. For new energy vehicles there will be the J7 PHEV and J8 PHEV, meanwhile in robotic innovation there is the Mornine robot, which integrates smart solutions in our mobility ecosystem," said Rifkie Setiawan, Head of Brand Department PT Chery Sales Indonesia.

Leading a New Era of New Energy Vehicles

OMODA & JAECOO will introduce a range of powerful new energy products, including J7 PHEV and J8 PHEV. J7 PHEV is an off-road vehicle with revolutionary new energy technology, which has the advantages of a variety of power modes, energy conservation, safety, four-wheel off-road capability, and smart technology to support an outdoor lifestyle. Meanwhile, the J8 PHEV is here for hybrid off-road vehicle consumers with the latest third generation hybrid platform from JAECOO. Both the J7 PHEV and J8 PHEV are poised to become leaders in the new energy off-road market segment by exceeding consumer expectations in the hybrid market.

Building a Future Ecosystem that Can Be Personalized According to Consumer Needs

OMODA & JAECOO's vision to go beyond the boundaries of the automotive industry is realized by building ecological relationships that are not just a means of transportation. Various innovations have been carried out to develop a series of ecological systems, including technology, fashion and Off-Road Life. These various ecological systems will provide a more personalized and connected experience to each consumer.

Entering a New Era of Robotics with Mornine

The next innovation is from Chery Automobile, the parent company of OMODA & JAECOO, in the form of robotic technology resulting from collaboration with the technology company AiMOGA. This robot, named Mornine, is the world's first walking bionic robot. which has an electrical energy source and is designed as an intelligent bipedal robot in human form. The presence of Mornine will be a significant step in entering a new era of robotics that will take the human-machine interaction experience to a higher level.

Three Main Functions of Mornine

OMODA & JAECOO have designed a strategy for Mornine to be able to fulfill three functions that will help consumers in their daily lives, namely:

  • Smart retail store waiters who will provide the help and information consumers need. The second function is as a wise friend in life
  • A wise friend in household life who will help users in various daily tasks as an intelligent household assistant.
  • Development and optimization for specific application scenarios. In this function, Mornine has the ability to meet specific needs in various industries and environments.
